HIKE DESTINATION: Cross-country skiing: Urabandai, Fukushima

TRANSPORT DETAILS:

 Car: Joban and Ban-etsu Expressways - detailed map from Ban-etsu Expressway is on the Maverick (lodge) pamphlet. Travel time: approx. 4 hours each way. Departed 7-11 at 7:30 AM - arrived at Maverick around 11:30 AM.

MAPS/GUIDEBOOKS:

 promotional pamphlets

HIKE DESCRIPTION:

 Cross country skiing in Urabandai area. There are some trails but you can also go skiing on lakes in the area.

ACCOMMODATION DETAILS:

Maverick Lodge (highly recommended): nice place; good food; exceptional service.
phone: (0241) 32-2016
fax: (0241) 32-2935
email: maverick@mxg.meshnet.or.jp

ONSEN DETAILS:

There are several onsens in the area. We tried the onsen at the Urabandai Nekoma Hotel (highly recommended); exceptional rotemburo; only 1000 yen - towels, soap and shampoo, combs, razors provided; close to Maverick. Phone: (0241) 37-1111
http://www.mwnet.or.jp/resort/nekoma/index.html

NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS:

10

WEATHER:

 Fine

OTHER COMMENTS:

The prepared trails are easy enough for beginners and lake skiing is not at all difficult. Those looking for a more challenging route would have to research other trails
