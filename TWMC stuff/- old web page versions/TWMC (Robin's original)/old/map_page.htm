Find out where we are going, or where we have been. There are 2 methods to view the maps of Japan:

1 - click on the images below which link to higher resolution pages. These images are of the whole of Japan, and some mountains are marked. Good for general viewing and seeing how far away from Tsukuba City it is.

2 - click on the places we have been or will go via the schedule table below. Please click on the place where we have been to / will goto, and an image of that locality (in better detail than option 1 above) will load. We have tried to get the whole of that prefecture into the image and a few mountain names too.

1 - low resolution map of Japan, broken up into sections.

Click on any section of Japan you are interested in and this will link to a larger picture of that area which you should be able to figure where we have been and roughly where it is placed in Japan.
