TWMC 2010 Summer Walks Schedule

Dates, short descriptions and the organizers name are given for each walk (### denotes that data is still awaited from the organizer). Please feel free to contact the organizer for joining up and questions, just click on the name to send e-mail. Important notice: Please note that the actual date may be changed to another weekend if the weather is bad, or if the organizer (who are all volunteers) is unable to organize it on that day. Generally walks are announced over the mailing list about two weeks ahead. Latest updates (of weather and participants) are sent over the mailing list (and not updated on the web).
